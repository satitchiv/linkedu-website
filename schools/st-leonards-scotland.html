ลักษณะโรงเรียนSchool Character
St Leonards is a pioneering IB school founded in 1877 as one of the first schools in the world to offer girls an education equal to boys, and today is the first UK school to offer all four IB programmes across its continuum. Set in the historic seaside town of St Andrews with its own golf academy, the school has an internationally-minded yet distinctively Scottish character built on innovation, cosmopolitan diversity, and a tradition of firsts.St Leonards is a pioneering IB school founded in 1877 as one of the first schools in the world to offer girls an education equal to boys, and today is the first UK school to offer all four IB programmes across its continuum. Set in the historic seaside town of St Andrews with its own golf academy, the school has an internationally-minded yet distinctively Scottish character built on innovation, cosmopolitan diversity, and a tradition of firsts.

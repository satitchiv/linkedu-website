Millfield vs Rossall: Sports Scholarship Comparison

Millfield and Rossall are two of the UK's premier boarding schools renowned for their exceptional sports programs and scholarship opportunities. This tool helps you understand which school might be the best fit for your specific sport and level.

Millfield School

Location: Somerset, England

Strengths: Elite sports facilities, world-class coaching, strong track record of producing professional athletes and Olympians. Offers scholarships from Year 9 and Year 12.

Best for: Athletes aiming for national/international recognition, especially in individual sports like swimming, athletics, golf, and tennis. Highly competitive scholarships.

Annual Boarding Fees (Year 9+):

GBP 57,132

THB 2,570,940 (GBP x 45)

*Note: Private school fees will have 20% VAT added from January 2025. This applies to Millfield. State boarding schools (approx. GBP 10,000-19,000/year) are VAT exempt.

Rossall School

Location: Lancashire, England

Strengths: Generous scholarship awards, a supportive environment for sports development, strong programs in football, golf (on-site links course), and a wide range of team and individual sports. Offers scholarships from Year 7, Year 9, and Year 12.

Best for: Athletes looking for excellent development pathways, often more accessible for strong regional-level athletes progressing to national. Strong community feel.

Annual Boarding Fees (Year 9+):

GBP 54,648

THB 2,459,160 (GBP x 45)

*Note: Private school fees will have 20% VAT added from January 2025. This applies to Rossall. State boarding schools (approx. GBP 10,000-19,000/year) are VAT exempt.

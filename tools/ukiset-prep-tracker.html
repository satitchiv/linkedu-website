What is UKISET?
(UKISET คืออะไร?)

The UK Independent Schools' Entry Test (UKISET) is a comprehensive online assessment used by many leading independent schools in the UK to evaluate international students for admission. It assesses a student's academic potential in key areas, along with their English language proficiency.

UKISET ช่วยให้โรงเรียนในสหราชอาณาจักรสามารถประเมินความสามารถทางวิชาการและทักษะภาษาอังกฤษของนักเรียนต่างชาติได้อย่างครอบคลุม โดยเฉพาะอย่างยิ่งสำหรับนักเรียนที่ต้องการเข้าเรียนในโรงเรียนเอกชนชั้นนำ

What UKISET Tests: English, Maths, Reasoning
(UKISET ทดสอบอะไรบ้าง: ภาษาอังกฤษ, คณิตศาสตร์, การคิดวิเคราะห์)

UKISET assesses students across several key modules to provide a holistic view of their abilities:

1. English
(ภาษาอังกฤษ)

Evaluates reading comprehension, grammar, vocabulary, and writing skills. It's crucial for academic success in an English-speaking environment.

  • Reading Comprehension: Understanding complex texts.
  • Grammar & Vocabulary: Correct usage and breadth of words.
  • Writing: Ability to express ideas clearly and coherently.

Sample Question: Read a passage and answer multiple-choice questions about its main idea, supporting details, and author's tone.

2. Mathematics
(คณิตศาสตร์)

Covers core mathematical concepts appropriate for the student's age group, including arithmetic, algebra, and geometry. Emphasis is on problem-solving.

  • Number: Arithmetic, fractions, decimals, percentages.
  • Algebra: Basic equations, expressions.
  • Geometry: Shapes, angles, area, volume.
  • Data Handling: Interpreting charts and graphs.

Sample Question: If a shirt costs £25 and is on sale for 20% off, how much does it cost now?

3. Reasoning
(การคิดวิเคราะห์)

Assesses logical thinking and problem-solving abilities, independent of learned knowledge. This includes verbal, non-verbal, and spatial reasoning.

  • Verbal Reasoning: Understanding relationships between words, analogies.
  • Non-Verbal Reasoning: Identifying patterns and relationships in abstract figures.
  • Spatial Reasoning: Visualising and manipulating shapes in space.

Sample Question: Which shape comes next in the sequence? (Followed by a series of abstract patterns).

UKISET Score Guide
(แนวทางการประเมินคะแนน UKISET)

UKISET scores are reported as standardised scores and percentiles. While specific school requirements vary, here's a general guide to interpreting scores:

Outstanding
(ยอดเยี่ยม)

Percentile: 85-100%
Indicates exceptional academic potential and English proficiency. Often sought by highly selective, top-tier schools.

Excellent
(ดีเลิศ)

Percentile: 70-84%
Demonstrates strong abilities. Suitable for many selective and reputable independent schools.

Good
(ดี)

Percentile: 55-69%
Shows solid foundational skills. Many good schools will consider students in this range, potentially with additional support for English.

Developing
(กำลังพัฒนา)

Percentile: Below 55%
Suggests areas for improvement. May require intensive preparation or consideration of schools with less emphasis on UKISET as a primary assessment.

Note: UKISET scores are just one part of the application process. Schools also consider school reports, interviews, and other entrance exams.

School Comparison / เปรียบเทียบโรงเรียน

Oxford International College (OIC)

  • Fees (Boarding): GBP 35,075 / THB 1,578,375
  • A-Level A*-A: 91% (often highest in UK)
  • Oxbridge Offers: 13 (typical per year)
  • Key Focus: Highly academic, intensive Oxbridge preparation, excellent progression to top universities.

Cardiff Sixth Form College

  • Fees (Boarding): GBP 62,095 / THB 2,794,275
  • UK Ranking: #1 UK (often cited by league tables)
  • Oxbridge Offers: 12 (2024 intake)
  • Key Focus: Top academic performance, outstanding university progression, 50% scholarship opportunities available.

Concord College

  • Fees (Boarding): GBP 61,764 / THB 2,779,380
  • A-Level A*-A: 74%
  • Oxbridge Offers: 103 (historically, over many years)
  • Key Focus: Strong academic tradition, well-rounded boarding experience, long-standing Oxbridge success.
